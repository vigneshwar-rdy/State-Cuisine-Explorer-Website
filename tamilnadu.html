TAMIL NADU

"Dive into the Rich Coastal Cuisine of Tamil Nadu!"

      
Sambar 1
A lentil-based stew that is a staple of Tamil cuisine. It is often served with idli, dosa, or rice.
      
Paruppu payasam 2
Paruppu payasam is a South Indian dessert made from lentils, milk, and sugar. It is a popular dish during festivals and special occasions. Paruppu payasam is also a good source of protein and fiber.
      
Pongal 3
A rice dish that is cooked in milk and water. It is often served with ghee, cashews, and raisins.
      
Chettinad chicken 4
Chettinad chicken is a delicious and flavorful dish that is native to the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu, India. It is made with chicken that is marinated in a blend of spices, including coriander, cumin, turmeric, chili peppers, and coconut, and then cooked in a gravy.
      
Idli 5
A steamed rice cake made from a fermented batter of rice and urad dal. It is often served with sambar and chutney.
