Trending Famous Food in 2023

Vada Pav 1
A fried mashed potato patty, sandwiched in a soft bun and served with a generous helping of chutney. It is a popular street food snack all over India.
Chicken Tikka Masala 6
A dish of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. It is a popular dish in North India and is often served with naan or rice.
Dosa 2
A thin pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It can be served plain or with a variety of fillings, such as potato masala, cheese, or chutney.
Biryani 7
A rice dish cooked with meat, vegetables, and spices. It is a popular dish all over India and comes in many different varieties.
Idli 3
A steamed cake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is a popular breakfast dish in South India.
Gulab Jamun 8
A sweet dish made from milk solids, sugar, and rose water. It is a popular dessert in India and is often served with rabdi or ice cream.
Samosa 4
A fried pastry filled with spiced potatoes, peas, and herbs. It is a popular snack food all over India.
Jalebi 9
A sweet dish made from a batter of flour and sugar, then deep-fried and soaked in syrup. It is a popular dessert in India and is often served with rabdi or ice cream.
Palak Paneer 5
A dish of spinach cooked with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) in a creamy tomato-based sauce. It is a popular vegetarian dish in North India.
Rasmalai 10
A sweet dish made from cottage cheese (paneer) soaked in a syrup flavored with cardamom and saffron. It is a popular dessert in India and is often served with rabri or ice cream.
